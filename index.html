    About Me

    SHOW-N (C.M.B), a Nigerian artist born in Imbitol Ogbaku, Imo State. My sound fuses hip-hop, reggae, dancehall, and R&B with melodic vocals that connect deeply with listeners. Starting music at 16, I’ve dropped powerful singles like "Stay Close," "Nobody Stand," and "Drive" — making waves across Nigeria. With performances across Lagos and Imo, I continue to grow as a voice of modern African music culture.
